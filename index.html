About me

 I am a Machine Learning Scientist at Amazon in Berlin (Germany).

Before joining Amazon I have been PhD Student in Applied Mathematics ("MASSC"), at University of Milan (Italy) under the supervision of Prof. Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi. During my PhD I also had the the opportunity to collaborate closely with Prof. Claudio Gentile and Fabio Vitale.

Something that you cannot find on my LinkedIn profile: some my jokes have been selected for this book.
